Background

Hi, I'm Joshua Sizer, a student at Wissahickon High School. I live in the suburbs of Philiadelphia in a town called Ambler. Born in South Africa, I had the opportunity to migrate to the United States when I was a few months old. My father, then working at Rohm and Haas as a software developer, has influenced and inspired both my brother's and my own passion for science, technology, and more specifically, computer science.

Interests

Technology

From the time I was a few years old, I would ask for Star Wars lego sets as birthday gifts. The idea that building blocks could come together to create something greater than the sum of their parts was exciting to me. I enrolled in our high school's FRC team, Team 341 Miss Daisy as soon as I was able. Throughout my years on the team, I have learned and been inspired by STEM. More important than that, Miss Daisy has taught me how to teach, lead, and listen.
