About

Here at RPM Consulting of Texas, we take care of it all for our clients.

From our initial cost analysis of your water and sewer billing history, we put together a plan that will show you the savings our monitoring and reporting services can deliver for your business. We will furnish and install utility grade smart meters and monitor your sites remotely.

With daily readings, we will create reports and alerts, and submit your evaporation credit requests to local utilities.

With annual reports for each site, we ensure that those credits come back to your bottom line.
