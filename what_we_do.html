What We Do

Our goal here at RPM Consulting of Texas is to give you superior service, knowledge, and help reduce your operating costs.

Our clients tell us that apart from the savings they receive, the best thing about working with us is they do not have to worry about the details – monitoring, reporting, or technical issues – because we truly take care of it all for them.

We perform a 6-step process:

  • ANALYZE

    We analyze your current water and sewer volumes and costs, and then create a plan to move forward.

  • INSTALL

    We install utility grade, smart meters and stand-alone cellular transmitters on cooling towers, irrigation systems or condensers.

  • MONITOR

    Using our smart technology, we remotely monitor each site. This also allows us to make sure that each cooling tower is operating properly. We are able to identify overflows, stuck fill valves, faulty blow-downs, and alert your operations teams of problems in the field.

  • REPORT

    We manage the reporting of sub-meter readings to your water or sewer utility.

  • SAVE

    You will receive a sewer fee credit that goes back to your businesses’ bottom line. Our monitoring services can save water and prevent waste due to mechanical problems.

  • CONFIRM

    We review your water and sewer bills once each year and confirm sewer credits were issued or posted to your accounts.
